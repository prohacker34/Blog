About This Blog

Hello! I'm Habert, the creator of this blog. I'm passionate about helping new drivers learn how to operate manual transmission vehicles with confidence.

This blog is designed for beginners who want step-by-step guidance on clutch control, gear shifting, reversing, and more. All the content is based on real-life experience and practical driving tips that are easy to follow.

Whether you're preparing for your driving test or just want to master stick shift driving, this site is here to support you on your journey.
