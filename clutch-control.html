Clutch Control and Gear Shifting

4. Shifting to Higher Gears

  • When RPM reaches around 2500–3000 or when the engine sounds high, take your foot off the gas.
  • Press the clutch down fully.
  • Move the gear stick to the next gear (1st to 2nd, 2nd to 3rd, etc.).
  • Release the clutch smoothly while gently pressing the gas again.

Always press the clutch before shifting, and shift in a smooth, controlled manner.

5. Slowing Down or Stopping

  • Take your foot off the gas.
  • Press the brake with your right foot.
  • If your speed drops below 10 km/h, press the clutch fully to avoid stalling.
  • Shift to neutral and release the clutch and brake once fully stopped.
