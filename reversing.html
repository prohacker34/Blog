Reversing and Parking a Manual Car

6. Reversing the Car

  • Fully press the clutch.
  • Shift into Reverse (R).
  • Gently press the gas and slowly release the clutch until you reach the biting point.
  • The car will start moving backward. Control your speed using the clutch and gas.

7. Parking a Manual Car

  • Stop the car completely.
  • Shift into first gear (or reverse) instead of neutral.
  • Pull up the handbrake.
  • Turn off the ignition.

Parking in gear prevents the car from rolling, especially on slopes.

Final Tips for Beginners

  • Practice in an empty lot or quiet road.
